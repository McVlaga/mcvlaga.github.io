Snipe

This is the application that gave me the most in terms of programming knowledge since I spent more than 500 hours on it in a span of 5 months. I learnt a lot of things about building iOS applications, but, more importantly, I learnt how to be a better programmer: how to read documentation and find things in it, how to read someone else's code, how to build upon someone else's code, and other things of that matter. I also learnt how important the architectural aspect of software is, and I learnt it the hard way, which is not that bad at all.

The application itself is just Yik Yak with pictures. There is a feed that looks kind of like the Instagram feed, but it has a different color palette (as you can see on the picture above). The feed is generated from pictures that were taken in the 5 mile radius around the user that sees the feed. It is totally anonymous: you do not need to register in order to post pictures or see them. You can put different masks on the picture you have taken such as the Batman's mask or a mask that is the Joker's face. The masks were made primarily for the pictures not to have faces in them. You can also shoot videos that are up to 10 seconds long. What is more, users are able to like or dislike pictures, and to comment on them.

My Personal Website

My very first website, and it did not turn out that bad. I wanted to make a simple programmer's portfolio website with a grain of good-looking user interface based on the principles of the material design. Even though there is nothing new that I created making this website (design-wise), I made it look appealing and user-friendly building upon something that was coined by the great minds of Google. Having said that, I encourage everyone who is not a designer to stick to some guidelines of either Google's material design or some other design language. I know that design and guidelines should not be put in the same sentence, but, unless you certainly know that you can make a good design, you should follow some rules created by someone who is credible in the world of design. That way the Internet can be more clean and good-looking.
